SPAM DETECTOR


What are spam?

Spam emails are digital junk sent in bulk that have destructive effects on mail servers and users. They consume memory space, communication bandwidth, and CPU power, causing unpleasant experiences. Spam mail also leads to fraudulence practices, harming individuals and organizations.
